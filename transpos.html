Transpos AR

  • by: Alex Ionkov

Transpos is an app written for iOS in Swift which would recognize images and display a 3D model over them. It uses a database which is updated when the app is opened. It was accepted to the gener8tor gAlpha incubator program in Fall 2019.

What Transpos offers:

  • Any picture can be paired with any 3D model
  • Both are stored in a database (Firebase)
  • This allows a variety of applications:
    • Room planning: print out a picture of furniture to get a AR model of it in your room
    • Food: AR menus where you can see portions and the food itself
    • Advertising: 3D models advertising anything
    • Museum: AR exhibit, see the sculpture as a 3D model
Focusing on an application is the next step
